Our Story

It all started in a small dorm room in 2023. Our founder, frustrated by the endless tabs needed to compare smartphone prices, wrote a simple script to do the heavy lifting. That script evolved into PriceFinder.

Today, we are a global team of engineers, data scientists, and design enthusiasts. We process terabytes of data daily to ensure that when you click "buy", you can do so with total confidence.
